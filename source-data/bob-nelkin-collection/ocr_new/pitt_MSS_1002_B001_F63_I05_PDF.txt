PROPOSED AMENDMENT TO SENATE BILL 94
Printer’s No. 1706
Section 1 is amended to read as follows:
A community residential facility means a facility providing residence for a minimum of three (3) and no more than eight (8) mentally ill persons who are not receiving treatment under Article III of the Mental Health Procedures Act, No. 143 of 1976 or who have not received treatment under Article IV of the Mental Health Procedures Act, No. 143 of 1976 and who are approved by County Mental Health and Mental Retardation as capable of residing in a group home and as receiving services from a local community mental health program; or dependent children; or mentally retarded persons; or physically handicapped persons; and providing residence for one (1) or two (2) other persons who provide services for such residents.
